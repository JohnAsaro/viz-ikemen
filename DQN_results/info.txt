In this folder is the model saved by BaseCallback after training an agent in the Viz Ikemen environment for 2048000 timesteps using a DQN. (model_2048000.zip)
Parameters can be found in parameters.txt.
In tensorboard/PPO_1 you can find the tensorboard logs from the training.
training_episodes_log.db is a log of each episodes winners and losers, organized by batch.
plot_episodes.ipynb plots training_episodes_log.db, and fits it to a n = 3 degree poly curve.
